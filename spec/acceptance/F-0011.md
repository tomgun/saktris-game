# F-0011: SpecialChessRules

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC1: Castling kingside
- King moves two squares toward h-file rook
- Rook moves to f-file
- Only if neither has moved, no pieces between, not through check

### AC2: Castling queenside
- King moves two squares toward a-file rook
- Rook moves to d-file
- Same conditions as kingside

### AC3: En passant
- Pawn captures enemy pawn that just moved two squares
- Captured pawn removed from board
- Only available immediately after double pawn move

### AC4: Pawn promotion
- Pawn reaching last rank must promote
- Choice of Queen, Rook, Bishop, or Knight
- Promotion dialog appears
