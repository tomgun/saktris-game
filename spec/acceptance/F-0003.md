# F-0003: PieceMovementRules

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC1: King movement
- King moves exactly one square in any direction (8 possible moves)
- King cannot move into check

### AC2: Queen movement
- Queen moves any number of squares horizontally, vertically, or diagonally
- Queen cannot jump over pieces

### AC3: Rook movement
- Rook moves any number of squares horizontally or vertically
- Rook cannot jump over pieces

### AC4: Bishop movement
- Bishop moves any number of squares diagonally
- Bishop cannot jump over pieces

### AC5: Knight movement
- Knight moves in L-shape (2+1 or 1+2 squares)
- Knight CAN jump over pieces

### AC6: Pawn movement
- Pawn moves forward one square (or two from starting position)
- Pawn captures diagonally
- Pawn cannot move backward

### AC7: Captures work
- Pieces can capture opponent pieces by moving to their square
- Captured piece is removed from board

### AC8: Legal move validation
- Only legal moves are allowed
- Illegal moves are rejected
