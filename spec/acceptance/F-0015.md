# F-0015: SoundFXAndThemes

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC1: Move sounds
- Sound plays on piece movement
- Per-piece sounds supported (optional)

### AC2: Capture sounds
- Distinct sound for captures

### AC3: Game event sounds
- Check sound
- Checkmate sound
- Stalemate sound
- Game start sound

### AC4: UI sounds
- Click sound
- Select sound

### AC5: Physics sounds
- Collision sound on piece collisions
- Triplet clear sound

### AC6: Volume control
- Master volume adjustable
- Music volume adjustable
- SFX volume adjustable

### AC7: Theme system
- Audio themes loadable from resources
- Classic theme included with all sounds

## Test Notes
- Manual audio testing
- Verify sounds play at appropriate times
